L'Expérience

More Than Coffee

01
🎵

Live Jazz Evenings

Every Friday and Saturday from 7pm, our resident jazz quartet transforms the café into a Parisian salon. Reserve a table early — they fill up fast.

02
🎨

Latte Art Classes

Join our master barista every Sunday morning for a 2-hour latte art workshop. Learn the tulip, rosette, and swan — and take your creation home.

03
🧑‍🍳

Patisserie Workshops

Learn to craft croissants and macarons with our head patissier, Chef Laurent. Small groups, big skills. Every second Tuesday.

04
📚

Private Tastings

Book a private cupping session for your team or group. Journey through 6 origins with our sommelier, pairing each bean with a macaron.

05
🎁

Gift Experiences

Give the gift of Maison Dorée. Our artisan gift boxes include single-origin beans, macarons, and an experience voucher. Ships across India.

06
🏢

Corporate Events

Hire our private dining room for up to 30 guests. Coffee catering, full patisserie spread, and a barista on-site for your event.

About

Born in Paris in 1987, reimagined for Mumbai in 2019. Maison Dorée is a sanctuary for those who believe coffee is a civilised pleasure, not a commodity.
