Our Story

A Passion for the
Perfect Cup

Born from a late-night conversation between two coffee obsessives, Aroma has grown from a tiny corner cart to one of the city's most beloved coffee destinations. We source our beans directly from ethical farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and India.

Every recipe is developed by our master baristas, who train for months before pulling their first shot. This isn't just coffee — it's a ritual, a relationship, a daily luxury you deserve.
